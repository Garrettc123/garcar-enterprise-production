# GAR-428: LinkedIn + Google Ads Campaign Strategy

## LinkedIn Campaign

### Targeting
- Job titles: Operations Manager, Owner, Founder, CEO, General Manager
- Industries: Construction, Roofing, Foundation Repair, Glass/Tinting, Field Services, SaaS
- Company size: 1-50 employees
- Location: United States (Texas priority)

### Ad Creative — Variant A (Lead Gen Form)
**Headline:** Stop doing manually what AI can do automatically
**Body:** Garcar Enterprise builds AI automation for service businesses. Invoice generation, client onboarding, lead follow-up — all automated. Book a free 30-min audit.
**CTA:** Book Free Audit

### Ad Creative — Variant B (Thought Leadership)
**Headline:** Why most agent frameworks will be rewritten in 18 months
**Body:** I built RHNS — a neuro-symbolic AI architecture with persistent causal memory. Now it runs the automation stack for service businesses. Technical founders: the repo is public.
**CTA:** Read the Architecture

### Budget
- Daily: $25-50
- Bid strategy: Target CPA $50 (audit booking)
- Duration: 30-day test, optimize weekly

### UTM Parameters
```
linkedin_variant_a: ?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=audit-offer&utm_content=variant-a
linkedin_variant_b: ?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=rhns-tech&utm_content=variant-b
```

## Google Ads Campaign

### Keywords (Exact + Phrase Match)
- [AI automation for small business]
- [automated invoicing system]
- [agentic OS]
- [business process automation service]
- [AI agent for roofing company]
- [automated client onboarding]
- "workflow automation consultant"
- "AI agent developer"

### Negative Keywords
- free, DIY, template, tutorial, course, certification

### Ad Groups
1. **Automation Services** — broad audience, ROI-focused copy
2. **RHNS / Tech** — developer/technical audience, architecture copy
3. **Vertical-Specific** — roofing, foundation, glass/tinting

### Landing Page Requirements
- Clear headline: "Automate your service business with AI"
- Sub-headline: ROI claim ("Recover 10+ hours/week")
- CTA above fold: Book Free Audit (Calendly)
- Social proof: 2-3 testimonials
- RHNS technical section for developer credibility
- Mobile-optimized (majority of traffic)

### Budget
- Daily: $25-50
- Bid: Target CPA $75
- Conversion goal: Calendly booking

### Weekly Monitoring Checklist
- [ ] CTR > 3% (pause underperformers)
- [ ] CPC trending
- [ ] Cost per lead vs target
- [ ] Conversion rate by ad group
- [ ] Negative keyword additions
